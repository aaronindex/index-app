# INDEX — Product Snapshot
Last updated: 2026-03

This document describes the **current product state of INDEX v2**.  
It is not philosophy, roadmap, or build notes.  
It reflects **what the product actually does today**.

---

# What INDEX Is

INDEX is a **structural ledger for thinking**.

Thinking happens elsewhere (GPT, docs, Slack, conversations).  
INDEX records the **signals that emerge from that thinking**.

Signals include:

- decisions
- tasks
- open loops
- highlights
- results

Over time these signals form **structure**:

- direction
- arcs
- phases
- milestones

INDEX does not attempt to capture thinking.  
It captures **the consequences of thinking**.

---

# Core Model

## Capture → Reduce → Structure

The product follows a simple loop.

1. Capture thinking
2. Reduce into signals
3. Structure emerges over time

---

## Signals

Signals are the core unit stored by INDEX.

Current signal types:

- Decision
- Task
- Highlight
- Loop
- Result

Signals have a status:

- active
- closed
- superseded
- invalidated

Signals accumulate into structural patterns across time.

---

# Key Product Surfaces

## Projects

Projects are **flat containers for thinking ingestion**.

Projects do not represent hierarchy or structure.  
They act as **anchors for capture and reduction**.

Users typically create projects for:

- a product
- a major initiative
- a topic of exploration

---

## Capture

Thinking can be captured through:

- Quick paste import (chat transcripts)
- Chrome extension quick capture
- manual paste

Captured material is stored for reduction.

---

## Reduce

Reduction extracts signals from captured thinking.

Signals may include:

- decisions
- tasks
- loops
- highlights

Tasks may be tagged:

- open loop
- commitment
- blocker

Reduction converts thinking into **structural signals**.

---

## Project → Read

The read surface shows the **distilled signals** for a project.

Signals can be:

- resolved
- invalidated
- superseded

Users can also **record results**, marking meaningful outcomes.

---

## Direction

Direction summarizes the **current trajectory** emerging from signals.

Direction is generated from:

- active decisions
- recent results
- structural signal changes

Direction is not manually written.

It emerges from structure.

---

## Structural Timeline

INDEX records structural events such as:

- signals created
- signals closed
- results recorded

The timeline shows **spacing of structural change**.

This reveals:

- acceleration
- drift
- pauses
- inflection points

Signals extracted

Thinking → Signals → Structure

# Chrome Extension

The Chrome extension allows quick capture.

Users highlight text and send it to INDEX.

This enables **capture where thinking happens**.

# Product Philosophy (Condensed)

INDEX is designed to be:

- quiet
- structural
- non-prescriptive
- reflective

It does not coach or advise.

It reveals **patterns in thinking over time**.

---

# What INDEX Is Not

INDEX is not:

- a note-taking tool
- a task manager
- a productivity system
- a journaling app
- an AI chat interface

It is a **ledger of structural signals**.
